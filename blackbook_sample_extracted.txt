Normal|Abstract
Normal|Blood Bond is an online blood donation system designed to bridge the gap between blood donors, seekers, hospitals, and blood banks. The system provides a platform where users can register as donors, request blood, and check blood availability efficiently. By leveraging ASP.NET MVC with Entity Framework and a Database-First approach, Blood Bond ensures seamless user interaction, data management, and optimized blood donation workflows.
Normal|The key features of Blood Bond include:
Normal|Donor & Seeker Management – Users can register, request blood, and update donation records.
Normal|Blood Bank & Hospital Integration – Hospitals and blood banks can manage inventory, requests, and stock availability.
Normal|Efficient Blood Request Matching – Seeker requests are matched with available donors or blood banks based on required blood type and location.
Normal|Secure Authentication & Role-based Access – Ensures authorized access for donors, seekers, hospitals, and administrators.
Normal|Request & Response Management – Allows seekers and hospitals to send, accept, or reject blood donation requests.
Normal|Blood Bond aims to reduce blood shortages and streamline the donation process by providing a centralized platform for managing blood requests and donor availability efficiently.
